The Boy In Striped Pyjamas

Review



The Boy In The Striped Pyjamas is a very worthwhile read for children aged 8-14 it's composition is very easy to understand and is truly heartfelt by the reader just enough information to make the reader guess what is happening in the story but enough to keep the reader interested.

Rating:7/10
